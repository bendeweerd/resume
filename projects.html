Projects

Luminous Display Pedestal

This was a hybrid woodshop and Arduino/embedded systems project. I constructed the table in the FHS woodshop, then added the LED strips and controller that I programmed at home. The Arduino Sketch for the project had 398 lines of code, most of which I wrote from scratch.

Calvin University Mars Rover: 2019-2020

I am an active part of Calvin's Mars Rover Team. I designed, modeled, and 3D printed the "wrist" of the rover arm, achieving the 4th and 5th degrees of freedom.

This Website

I've enjoyed building this website, and I've learned a lot from doing it. This is the 3rd iteration; the first was pure HTML and the second incorporated some CSS. This version also uses the Bootstrap library to make the page visually cohesive on different screen sizes.

Feathered-Leg Table

I constructed this table, which incorporates a unique leg design, in my sophomore year of high school. In one picture, I'm holding the state championship award for the table next to my shop teacher Rick Tank and friend Keegan.

3D-Printed Mountain Range Reliefs

This hybrid project combined a 3D-printed mountain range with a simple cherry base. My mother grew up next to this mountain range, and I made these as a gift for her and my aunt.
